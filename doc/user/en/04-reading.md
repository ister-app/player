# Reading

Ister isn't just for watching and listening — it has a full built-in reader for ebooks and comics, on every platform including Linux and Android TV.

## The epub reader

Open an ebook from its [book page](02-browsing.md) and it renders right in the app: clean typography, chapter by chapter. Swipe or use the arrow keys to turn pages, and open the table of contents to jump to any chapter.

![The epub reader](../images/en/epub-reader.png)

### Your reading position

Your position syncs to the server automatically as you read. Even better: for books that also have an audiobook, the reading and listening positions are kept in sync — finish a chapter on your commute by ear, open the book at home and continue reading from that exact spot, and vice versa.

## Read-aloud

Books that ship with narrated audio built into the ebook (EPUB 3 media overlays) can read themselves to you. Start read-aloud and the app plays the narration while highlighting the sentence being spoken, following along as the pages turn. Starting read-aloud pauses whatever else was playing.

## The comic reader

Comics get their own reader tuned for pages: swipe through page spreads, pinch to zoom, and your progress is remembered just like with books.

![The comic reader showing a page spread](../images/en/comic-reader.png)
